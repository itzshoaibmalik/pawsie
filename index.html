Frequently Asked Questions

What is the adoption process like?

The adoption process involves an application, home visit, and meeting with the dog. We ensure the best match for both you and your new companion.

How much does dog training cost?

Training costs vary based on the program and duration. Basic obedience starts at $200, while advanced training can range from $500-$1000.

Do you offer pet insurance?

Yes, we partner with leading pet insurance providers to offer comprehensive coverage plans starting at $25/month.

What if my dog has health issues?

Our 24/7 vet service is available for emergencies, and we have a network of specialists for ongoing health management.
